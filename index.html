Profile

About

I am an undergraduate student in Computer Science at Fuyang Normal University. My research experience centers on nonlinear control and path-tracking control for autonomous agricultural vehicles. I have worked on adaptive super-twisting, second-order, and higher-order sliding-mode control, together with MATLAB-based simulation and finite-time stability analysis.

I am currently broadening my background toward learning-based control, multimodal learning, and embodied intelligence, with the long-term goal of connecting rigorous control methods with intelligent autonomous systems.

I am interested in graduate research opportunities for Fall 2027.
