Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 13, 2026

What the public map shows

The homepage displays a third-party visitor map provided by whos.amung.us. It shows approximate country- or region-level locations for current and some recent visits. The public website does not display a visitor's exact IP address, name, email address, or street address.

Data processed by the map provider

When the map loads, the provider receives standard request information such as the visitor's IP address, the page URL, and browser or operating-system details. The provider states that it anonymizes IP addresses by zeroing the final octet and aggregates non-personally-identifiable visit data after approximately 20 days. Its service and advertising partners may use cookies or similar technology.

Read the provider's privacy policy and terms.

Cumulative page-load counter

The footer also loads a Hits badge to show a cumulative page-load count. It is separate from the recent visitor map, so the two displays use different counting methods and should not be expected to match.

Your choice

You can prevent these third-party analytics requests by blocking third-party content or cookies in your browser. If blocked, the map or counter may not load and the visit will not appear in those displays.
